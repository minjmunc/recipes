Simple Homemade Dreamsicles

Description

A nice soothing dessert/snack to help cool you down

Ingredients

  • 2 cups orange sherbet, slightly softened
  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream, slightly softened
  • 1 cup fresh orange juice
  • 2/3 cup simple syrup
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream

Steps

  1. Combine sherbet and ice cream in a large ziplock plastic bag. Massage the bag to slightly mix
    the 2 colors. Make a 3/4-inch cut in the bottom corner of the bag. Squeeze about 3 tablespoons of sherbet mixture
    into each of 10 (3-oz) popsicle molds.

  2. Stir together orange juice, simple syrup, and cream in a liquid measuring cup. Pour juice mixture
    into popsicle molds allowing liquid to fill in any remaining air pockets of the ice cream. Insert popsicle
    sticks, and freeze for at least 8 hours.
